Our Services

Bike tuning 85$

Bike tuning is the detailed and precise adjustment and optimization of a bicycle's components to significantly enhance its performance, comfort, and efficiency. This specialized service involves fine-tuning gears for smoother shifting, precisely adjusting suspension settings for improved control, and calibrating brakes for optimal safety and responsiveness. By customizing the bike to fit the rider's specific needs and preferences, bike tuning ensures a superior, tailored, and more personalized cycling experience, improving both the enjoyment and effectiveness of each ride.

Bike fitting $60

Bike fitting is a comprehensive process designed to adjust and tailor a bicycle to the specific body measurements, riding style, and goals of the cyclist. It involves a detailed assessment of the rider's posture, flexibility, and biomechanics to optimize the bike's configuration—such as saddle height, handlebar position, and pedal alignment. This personalized adjustment aims to enhance comfort, improve efficiency, and prevent injuries, ensuring a harmonious relationship between the cyclist and their bike for a more enjoyable and effective riding experience.

Bike reperation 150$

Bike repair refers to the process of diagnosing and fixing mechanical issues with a bicycle, ensuring it operates smoothly and safely. This service can range from basic maintenance tasks, such as adjusting brakes and gears, fixing punctures, and replacing worn-out parts, to more complex repairs like overhauling bearings or straightening bent frames. Professional bike repair aims to restore the bicycle to its optimal condition, enhancing its performance and longevity, and providing the cyclist with peace of mind and reliability in their riding experience.
